✓ Warbler side ships — Raven side needs pass

Raven side went too aerial/simulation — reads like a map rather than a felt POV. Needs more sky, lower angle, sense of the bird in flight through space rather than above it. The crow is correctly small in frame but the camera angle is wrong.

Raven POV: camera behind and slightly below the raven at altitude, angled forward — we see the raven's wings in the lower portion of frame, the suburban landscape spreading below and ahead. Sky fills 40% of frame. The bird is flying through space, not surveying from above. Same neighborhood assets as warbler panel, same scale differential, different felt angle.
